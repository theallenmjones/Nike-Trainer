ATLANTA

We are all meant to be runners and every run has a purpose. Together, Nike and the great citizens of Atlanta, GA are running to help fight against the disadvantages that are sometimes thrown our way in life. With every kilometer ran by each nike runner within 30 days, a donation of $1 will be contributed to those who need out help.
